About

nice to meet you

I am a first-year master's student at the University of Michigan School of Information studying UX/HCI and data science. I joinded this program becuase of my love for design, passion for research, and overall interest in the ways humans interact with technology.

I have a B.S. in sociology from UofM which I find useful in my everyday work as an aspiring designer, researcher, and developer. I believe it is imperative to view human-computer interaction through a sociological lens to truly understand how technology is impacting the user both negatively and positively.

In my free time, I enjoy photography, reading, cooking, and spending time with loved ones.

Send me an email if you want to learn more about my work...or just to chat about the world!
