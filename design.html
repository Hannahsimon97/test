ActiVote

an interactive tool that connects people with political engagement opportunities

Big Problem

Voters do not have the technology to connect them with accurate information about nearby civic engagement opportunities

  1. Goal: design a service for young voters that facilitates the process of engaging in political involvement activities by consolidating information on accessible, local resources.

  2. Interactive tool: social media app where each user has a calendar to upload which political involvement events they will attend. The service will facilitate students' interaction with political engagement by providing information about events based on geographic location.

  3. Target audience: young voters age 18-28

Needfinding

Interviews with 20+ users allowed me to better understand the issue at large and how to carry out my design according to the needs of my target audience

  1. Social Media Young people use social media apps (twitter, instagram, facebook) for news

  2. Information Overload People experience information overload on social media platforms

  3. Community People are more likely to attend event with friends

  4. Political Behaviors People are less private about political beliefs in 2020

  5. Barriers to Involvement Time was a key factor in whether people engaged in politics

Prototype

As users enter their personal information to create their account, they are shown a civic engagement quiz which would gather data on what content to show them based on political preferences.

Users can update their personal profile whenever they choose with content such as a profile picture, profession, political identity, passion project, etc.

Users can browse the "news feed" section allows user to browse events nearby.

Users can click on an event to view more information and add the event to their personal calendar.

Users can view others' personal profiles and calendars to see which events their friends are planning to attend.

Users can send events to friends with added personal messages.

Users can post about events and interact with posts by others.

ActiVote aims to inspire a new generation of politically engaged citizens.
